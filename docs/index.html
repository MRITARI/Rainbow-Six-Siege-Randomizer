R6 RANDOMIZER

Random Operator and Loadout Utility

Overview

The R6 Randomizer is your go-to companion for Rainbow Six Siege, helping you pick a random operator for Attackers or Defenders. It’s a quick way to keep your gameplay fresh and unpredictable, with full control over which operators are included.

Downloads

Key Features

  • Randomize operator for Attack/Defend.
  • Persistent operator management (enabled/disabled).
  • Loadout randomizer (Primaries, Secondaries, Gadgets).
  • In-game overlay toggle (Shift + F5).

Troubleshooting

  • Must enable at least one operator.
  • Use borderless windowed mode for overlay.
  • Verify hotkey is not bound elsewhere.
